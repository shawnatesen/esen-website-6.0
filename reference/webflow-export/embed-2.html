「我從 40 歲開始每年做健康檢查，

"I've had an annual health check every year since I turned 40. I'm not someone with a long list of red marks."

這是鄒開蓮 Rose 的開場白。前 Yahoo！台灣總經理，後升任 Verizon Media 國際事業董事總經理，麾下近 2,000 人的團隊橫跨亞太、歐洲與拉丁美洲。她一年出差超過 20 趟，與時差為伍是日常。

按照傳統健檢的判讀邏輯，Rose 是資優生。報告上的紅字屈指可數，每一年都是綠燈。

但 ĒSEN 替她重新讀過報告之後，畫面完全不同。

That's how Rose Tsou opens the conversation. Former General Manager of Yahoo! Taiwan, later Managing Director of Verizon Media's international business, leading a team of nearly 2,000 across Asia-Pacific, Europe, and Latin America. Twenty-plus business trips a year. Living with jet lag as a baseline. By the standards of conventional health checks, Rose was the model student. Red marks were few; year after year, the report was green. Then ĒSEN re-read her reports. The picture changed completely.

她的皮質醇指數長期偏高——這項數值，在多數標準健檢中根本不會被測量，即便測了，也常被當作「壓力大，正常」一筆帶過。但皮質醇的長期升高，是觸發慢性低度發炎的已知機制之一，而慢性低度發炎，正是當代長壽醫學認為加速老化的核心驅動力。

更關鍵的是 VO2Max。Rose 過去從未測過這項指標。「以前覺得運動只是為了線條，從沒把運動跟肺活量及 longevity 思考在一起過。」評估結果出來後，ĒSEN 為她規劃了每週 150 分鐘的有氧處方，並持續追蹤她的肺活量、肌力與身體組成。

報告上的紅字沒有增加，但 Rose 對自己身體的理解，徹底重組了。

Her cortisol had been elevated for years — a marker most standard health checks don't measure, and when they do, it's often dismissed as "high stress, normal." But sustained cortisol elevation is one of the known triggers of chronic low-grade inflammation, which longevity medicine now recognizes as a core driver of accelerated aging. More critical still: VO2Max. Rose had never been tested for it. "I used to think exercise was about body shape — I'd never connected it to lung capacity or longevity." After the assessment, ĒSEN designed a 150-minute weekly aerobic protocol and tracked her cardiorespiratory fitness, strength, and body composition over time. The number of red marks on her report didn't increase. But Rose's understanding of her own body was completely rebuilt.
